About the Event

Hacktoberfest, the month-long festival of supporting and contributing to open-source, is back again this year. We are excited to share with you guys that CBIT will be hosting a 24-hour online hackathon as part of it, called CBIT Hacktoberfest Hackathon. Hacktoberfest is a virtual festival to celebrate open source contributions presented by DigitalOcean, Intel and DEV. It is the easiest way to get into open source! We are hosting the Hacktoberfest on the 29th and 30tt of October 2021.
